Insurance Coverage

Dr. Zhu is the preferred provider for most insurance plans, such as Blue Cross Blue Shield, United healthcare, Cigna, Great-West, PHCS, etc.

If your insurance covers Acupuncture we will be happy to file the claim for you. We will collect any co-pay/co-insurance at the time of your appointment.

Insurance coverage is not always guaranteed. Each individual plan has different benefits and we will verify what your insurance covers for you. In order to assist, please email the following items to us or call our office at 512-457-0886 before your first appointment.

·    Your full name and date of birth.
·    Your insurance name and member ID which is on the front of your insurance card.
·    Insurance provider phone number that is on the back of your insurance card.
·    If the insurance policy holder is someone other than yourself, we will need their name and date of birth.
Medicare or Medicaid plans exclude acupuncture coverage.

Several payment plans and valuable pre-paid package are available; please let us know if finances are restricted so that we can tailor a plan to fit your needs.

We accept checks and all major credit cards.
